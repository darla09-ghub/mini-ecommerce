About Darla & Scents

Darla & Scents is a Kenyan perfume company dedicated to crafting unique and luxurious fragrances. Each perfume is designed to tell a story, evoke emotions, and enhance personal style. Our mission is to make exquisite scents accessible while maintaining exceptional quality.
